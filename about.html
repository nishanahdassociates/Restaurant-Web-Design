Our Story

A journey of passion, craftsmanship, and culinary excellence that began with a simple dream: to create unforgettable dining experiences.

Culinary Philosophy

At Savoir, we believe that dining is more than sustenance—it's an art form that engages all senses. Our philosophy centers on three pillars: exceptional ingredients, innovative techniques, and warm hospitality.

We partner directly with local farms, artisanal producers, and sustainable fisheries to source the finest seasonal ingredients. Each dish is crafted to tell a story, combining classical French techniques with modern creativity.

Our commitment extends beyond the plate to create an atmosphere where every guest feels welcomed, valued, and inspired. This is dining as it should be—an celebration of life's finest pleasures.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to culinary excellence, follow our path of growth and recognition.

2018 - The Beginning

Chef Alexandre Dubois opens Savoir with a vision to bring refined French cuisine to the heart of the city, starting with just 12 tables and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

2019 - First Recognition

Awarded "Best New Restaurant" by the Culinary Institute and receives our first Michelin star recommendation, establishing Savoir as a destination for fine dining.

2021 - Sustainability Focus

Launch our farm-to-table program, partnering directly with local organic farms and implementing zero-waste practices in our kitchen operations.

2023 - Michelin Star

Awarded our first Michelin star, recognizing our dedication to culinary excellence and innovative approach to modern French cuisine.

2024 - Present Day

Continuing to evolve and innovate, with a team of 25 culinary professionals serving over 200 guests nightly while maintaining our commitment to excellence.
